JULIE HERRMANN

IDEAS120 PORTFOLIO



PROJECT 1: To create this webpage, I took the last text message that was sent to me and searched each piece of the sentence on Google Images individually to generate an abstract poem accompanied by seemingly unrelated pictures, even though they are connected.

PROJECT 2: Using an appropriated video of birds pecking at food created for the purpose of entertaining cats, I utilized the background function on Zoom to record an illusionary scenario of myself interacting with my environment

PROJECT 3: Through the web-based application Twine, I made a non-linear narrative game simulating the choices one is confronted with when deciding whether it is safe to go out during a pandemic. This game has 6 alternate endings, all of which lead to an unfortunate result.
